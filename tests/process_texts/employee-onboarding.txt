When a new employee joins the company, HR first creates their profile in the HR system and sends a welcome email with login credentials. The IT department is notified automatically and sets up a laptop, installs required software, and grants access to internal tools based on the employee's role.

On the first day, the employee attends an orientation session held by HR. Afterwards, their line manager assigns a buddy from the team and schedules a series of onboarding meetings with key stakeholders for the first two weeks.

The employee completes mandatory compliance training within the first five days. Once all training modules are marked complete, HR closes the onboarding checklist and the employee is considered fully onboarded.
